About Nicolas Mar

Nicolás María, aka Nicolas Mar, was born in June 6, 1993 in Mar del Plata (Buenos Aires), Argentina. He starts in music with his guitar at 12 . Over time, he participates in different groups of rock, alternative pop and sessionist in tribute groups. At the age of 16, he began producing electronic nights with friends in Mar del Plata, Córdoba and Buenos Aires, while introducing electronic sounds through synthesizers and producing live electroacoustic sounds. He defines himself as a "ghost producer" since he has constantly created and recorded an infinite number of unpublished musical works. He is established in the world of production, recording, mixing, acquiring growing knowledge. Shortly after, NICOLAS MAR took his identity in the under of Buenos Aires.

Defined at House, Deep House, Tech House & Minimal, the proposal adapts to the different requirements of the electronic scene, both at sunset and in nightclubs. He accompanies today local and international DJs the aesthetic quality of the show always synchronizes the art both in the sound and the image of its staging to give a more solid concept to your show.

Since 2018, Nicolas Mar has toured the Riviera Maya of Mexico, in cities with strong electronics development such as Tulum, Playa Del Carmen, Merida, Cancún and Isla Mujeres. By the end of 2019, he will begin his second international tour between Europe and Oceania. The vision of the dancefloor and the perfection of each set he made led him in a one year barely to reside in the Caribbean to play with both experienced local and international DJ's; Stefano Noferini, Davide's squillace, Marco Bailey, Nicola Cruz, Darin epsilon, D-formation, Okain, Leon (among others).
